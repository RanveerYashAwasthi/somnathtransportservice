# Design Context — Somnath Transport Service

## Target Audience
Ohio business owners, operations managers, logistics coordinators, and dispatch teams evaluating a freight/transport partner. They value reliability, clear pricing, and fast response over flashy design.

## Use Cases
- Evaluate whether Somnath can handle their freight needs (FTL, LTL, local, interstate)
- Request a quote for a specific shipment
- Call directly to get answers fast
- Confirm legitimacy and professionalism before committing

## Brand Personality
Trustworthy. Bold. Professional. Established. No-nonsense. The kind of company that shows up on time and doesn't over-promise.

## Aesthetic Direction
**Industrial editorial** — high contrast, typography-driven hierarchy, structured layouts. Navy dominant with orange used surgically on conversion points only. No cards, no boxes, no rounded corners. Rules and whitespace create groupings. Left-aligned, asymmetric compositions. Think Swiss/International style meeting freight industry toughness.

## Principles
1. Lead with credibility before decoration
2. Orange is reserved for primary CTAs only — never decorative
3. Structured, left-aligned, scannable layouts
4. Contact/quote paths visible in every viewport
5. No cards, no box-shadows, no border-radius — rules and typography create hierarchy
6. Vary spacing rhythm — tight groupings vs generous separations
7. Every word earns its place — no filler copy

## Typography
- **Display**: Barlow Condensed (500–800) — industrial, condensed, uppercase, reads like highway signage
- **Body**: Source Sans 3 (300–600) — clean US govt-style reading font, professional
- Fluid heading sizes via clamp() with balanced growth terms (e.g. `clamp(2.4rem, 4vw + 1rem, 4.5rem)`)
- Headings use `text-wrap: balance`, body text uses `text-wrap: pretty`
- Tight line-height on headings (1.05), generous on body (1.65)
- All headings uppercase via `text-transform: uppercase`

## Logo
- SVG mark: forward-pointing arrow/chevron with "ST" monogram
- Full wordmark: "SOMNATH" in Barlow Condensed + "TRANSPORT SERVICE" in Source Sans 3
- Navy primary, orange accent on S letterform
- Files: logo.svg (header wordmark), favicon.svg (ST monogram mark)

## Colors
- Navy #0A2342 (dominant — headings, footer, CTA bands)
- Orange #F57C00 (conversion only — primary buttons, key numbers)
- Off-white #FEFEFE (page background)
- Ink #1A1E26 (dense text)
- Text #3B4049 (body copy)
- Muted #6B7280 (secondary labels)
- Rule #D4D7DC (dividers)
