About Somnath Transport

Ohio-based. Owner-operated. Built on one principle—your freight is our reputation.

Our story

Somnath Transport Service started with a single truck and a straightforward idea: pick up freight on time, deliver it on time, and don't make excuses when things get hard. That idea hasn't changed.

Based in Columbus, Ohio, we've grown into a full-service freight company covering local same-day deliveries across every Ohio metro and long-haul interstate routes spanning all 48 contiguous states. Every mile has been driven by our own people, in our own trucks, under our own name.

We're not a broker. We don't subcontract your load to a stranger. When you call Somnath, you talk to the people who actually move your freight—and that's the way it should be.

What we stand for

Four principles that guide every load we move.

01

Reliability over everything

We quote a date, we hit that date. No "weather delays" on sunny days, no vague ETAs. If something changes, you hear about it before you have to ask.

02

Own the entire chain

Our trucks, our drivers, our dispatchers. When there's no middleman, there's no finger-pointing. One team, one standard, every load.

03

Transparent pricing

The quote we give is the price you pay. No fuel surcharge surprises, no hidden accessorials, no creative invoice line items. Freight is complicated enough.

04

Know the roads

We've run Ohio routes for years. We know the construction zones, the weigh stations, the fastest alternatives when I-71 backs up. Local knowledge is a competitive advantage.
